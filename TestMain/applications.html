Contempory Arts and Live Arts

This department takes mainstream events that are performed live and puts a unique twist to it.

Fine Arts and Digital Arts

×

Fine Arts and Digital Arts

This is an art/design based department that includes aesthetic art along with digital art events.

Volunteers will be responsible for handling the event as well as to assist the participants at the venue.

Speaking Arts and Literary Arts

×

Speaking Arts and Literary Arts

This is a department dealing with everything, from your pun offs to your word play events, etc.

Volunteers are required to help the management for the smooth running off the event at the venue.

Gaming and Sports

×

Gaming and Sports

This deals with video gaming along with indoor and outdoor sports activities.

Volunteers are required to help manage the teams, players, matches as well as to try and avoid any mishaps

Chaos

×

Chaos

This department consists of a systematic combination of various unusual events including talent hunt, fashion show and a few tricky, fun and exhilarating events.

Nukkad

Nukkad

This department brings to you a fascinating crossover with all the 6 event departments.

It's a merge of two art forms, thus cultivating a never seen before event. Musical talents along with dance, drama and improv theatre are all included in it.

Administration

Registration

×

Registration

Registration is responsible for managing participant registration, prior and on days of the fest.

They are also responsible for clarifying any doubts pertaining to any event or department of the fest.

Scoring

×

Scoring

The Scoring department is responsible for handling the score sheets of the events that are happening on the day & includes calculations.

Basic knowledge of excel is required.

Organisation

Security

×

Security

This is a department that operates like the armed forces. The Security department requires the most amount of manpower.

The department ensures a smooth functioning of the fest whilst trying to avoid any mishaps.

Logistics

×

Logistics

The Logistics department is divided into 3 sub departments - auditorium, court and the classrooms.

They outline the logistical setup and the layout of the events in the auditorium, court and the classrooms based on the event taking place.

Tech

×

Technicals

This is a department that will overlook all the requirements pertaining to audio, video and everything associated with setups and technical requirements for every event.

Entertainment

×

Entertainment

Artists and performances is what this department is made of.

Before the fest, this department is responsible for promoting the fest through their talent. On the day of the fest, they take care of the audience by keeping them entertained while they are waiting for the next performance. This department is also in-charge of the fillers.

Finance

×

Finance

Why is money called dough? Because we all knead it!

Therefore keeping a track of whether it comes in or goes out is the sole responsibility of the Finance department.

Public Relations

External Public Relations

×

External Public Relations

This department acts as a point of contact between invited colleges and the Mosaic Team.

It also includes being responsible for colleges and passing all vital information to the participants along with taking care of all needs of the participants.

Internal Public Relations

×

Internal Public Relations

Internal PR includes planning, executing and organization of various promotional activities, pre-events, PR activities, game days, CSR events and scoring PR for Mosaic.

Celebrity Management

×

Celebrity Management

This is a department that handles the judges and celebrities for all the various events being held.

It deals with calling the judges for the events, making sure all their requirements are fulfilled on the event days in conjunction with their pick up and drop, to and fro from the venue.

Hospitality

×

Hospitality

Hospitality focuses on the overall well-being of everyone present during the fest by providing assistance.

This department is responsible for providing and managing food and water for everyone. This department is also responsible for the lost and found as well as the first aid facilities.

Marketing

×

Marketing

This department is the base of the fest in terms of getting sponsors to run the various activities during the 4 days.

Volunteers will be the official representatives to the companies coming on board for the partnership of the fest.

Media

Digital Content

The Digital Content department handles social media in terms of what pictures go up, how they should be designed and relevant captions for them, writing articles for newspapers and proofreading them in conjunction with covering all the events and activities happening before and on the day of the fest on the digital platform.

The department also deals with networks(coding) and graphics.

Creatives

Creatives

The Creatives department is responsible for all things 'Creative'.

From posters to banners, to décor to art installations - this department has the herculean task of breathing life into art within the boundaries of the college.

Souvenirs

Souvenirs

This department makes sure that you have something more than just beautiful memories to take back home from Mosaic.

Volunteers are required to put their artistic talents to use.

Coverage

Coverage

It requires its volunteers to constantly be on their toes in order to get that one best shot that will contribute to the memories that the fest will create.
